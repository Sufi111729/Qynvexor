## Start with source quality
Compression works best when the original file is scanned clearly and exported with balanced settings.

## Choose the right profile
Use medium compression for internal sharing and higher quality profiles for printable records.

## Validate before publishing
Always review fonts, signatures, and tables before sending files to users.
